# Provider Logos

## Current Status
✅ Placeholder SVG logos are currently in place and working!

## How to Add Real Logos

To replace the placeholders with actual provider logos:

1. **Download official logos** from:
   - **Rapido**: Visit rapido.bike and save their logo
   - **Uber**: Visit uber.com/brand and download their logo
   - **Namma Yatri**: Visit nammayatri.in or their press kit

2. **Prepare the images**:
   - Recommended format: PNG with transparent background
   - Recommended size: 200x200 pixels (or any square dimension)
   - File names: `rapido.svg`, `uber.svg`, `namma_yatri.svg`

3. **Replace the files** in this directory

## Using Your Own Logos

If you have logo files, simply replace:
- `rapido.svg` → Replace with your Rapido logo
- `uber.svg` → Replace with your Uber logo  
- `namma_yatri.svg` → Replace with your Namma Yatri logo

The app will automatically use them! 🚀
